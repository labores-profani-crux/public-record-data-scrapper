# Executive Hiring Video Script
## UCC-MCA Intelligence Platform Portfolio Presentation

**Duration:** 3-5 minutes  
**Target Audience:** Non-technical executives, founders, hiring managers  
**Tone:** Clear, confident, intelligent, non-jargony  
**Framework:** Problem → Insight → Solution → Impact → Why Me

---

## 1. EXECUTIVE HOOK (0:00 - 0:30)

### Voiceover:
*"Every day, thousands of businesses file for financing. Hidden in those public records are patterns—signals that reveal which companies are thriving, which are struggling, and which ones are ready to buy again. The problem? This data is scattered across fifty different state websites, buried in legal jargon, and impossible to analyze at scale."*

### Visual Plan:
- [ON SCREEN: Animated map of USA with data points lighting up across states]
- [ON SCREEN: Close-up of complex legal document, zooming out to reveal thousands more]
- [TEXT OVERLAY: "50 States. 500,000+ Annual Filings. Zero Structure."]

### Purpose:
Immediately establish the problem's scope and the candidate's ability to identify high-value opportunities in complexity.

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Pacing:
- Speak at 150-160 words per minute
- Use natural pauses for emphasis
- Avoid rushed delivery—confidence requires space

### Tone:
- Conversational but professional
- Avoid overly scripted reading—sound like you're explaining to a colleague
- Emphasize key phrases: "decision-making problem," "competitive advantage," "compound in value"

### Visual Consistency:
- Use consistent color palette (navy, amber, cyan from PRD)
- Maintain clean, minimal design language
- Avoid cluttered screens—one concept per visual
- Text should be large, brief, and impactful

### Technical Choices:
- Export as 1080p or 4K video
- Use professional voiceover or clear audio recording
- Consider subtle background music (low, non-distracting)
- Closed captions recommended for accessibility
